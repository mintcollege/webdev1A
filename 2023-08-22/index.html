ACADEMIC TRACKS

MINT High offers academic strands in the areas of business, arts, and technology. These strands merge seamlessly with our college programs, eventually making it easier for our students to adjust to a higher level of education.

Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)

With special emphasis on creative and media industries, our students develop their knowledge and skills in entrepreneurship and integrated marketing communications. Students focus on the conceptualization, formulation, and commercialization of ideas into a profitable venture. This track encourages students to be content creators, leveraging the facilities that are available to them at MINT High.

Focus:

  • Creative and media industries
  • Integrated marketing communications
  • Content creation

Target Courses:

  1. Music Business ManagementA
  2. Marketing
  3. Entrepreneurship

Arts and Design

This track is an introduction to our key creative programs in multimedia arts, film, and theater. Our professional faculty facilitates projects that are aligned with current industry practices, allowing our students to experience real-world application of their skills. They are also trained to be experts at problem-solving, and collaboration. They are taught that professionals should work together to ensure that their processes are organized to come up with well-communicated output.

Focus:

  • Project-focused
  • Problem-solving
  • Collaboration

Target Courses:

  1. Multimedia Arts
  2. Film
  3. Theater Arts

General Academic

This track is an extension of core programs offered at MINT College. There is heavy emphasis on project-based learning, group study, and working in a highly collaborative work environment. To complement its modern mode of instruction, technology is at its core, with students having access to MINT’s state of the art laboratories.

Focus:

  • Modern approach to education
  • Alignment with MINT College’s programs
  • Technological integration

Target Courses:

  1. Information Technology
  2. Computer Science
